Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dante Tran ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the SnapScreen mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Privacy Highlights:
  • All screenshot processing happens entirely on your device
  • We do not upload, store, or access your images
  • Edited screenshots are saved only to your device
  • We use Firebase Analytics to improve the app experience

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:

  • Your Screenshots or Images: All image processing occurs entirely on your device. We never upload, access, store, or transmit your screenshots or any images you edit.
  • Personal Photos: Your photo library remains private and on your device.
  • Text Content: Any text in your screenshots is processed locally and never leaves your device.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the App. This may include:

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • General location (country/region level only)
  • App crashes and performance data

This data is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify you personally.

1.3 Purchase Information

When you make a purchase or subscribe, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive only:

  • Confirmation of your subscription status
  • Subscription type (weekly/yearly)
  • Subscription expiration date

We do not receive or store your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Verify your subscription status
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your edited screenshots and app preferences are stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Saved screenshot edits
  • App settings and preferences
  • Recent editing history

3.2 Analytics Data

Anonymous analytics data is stored securely on Firebase servers operated by Google. This data is retained according to Firebase's data retention policies.

4. Third-Party Services

SnapScreen uses the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Firebase Analytics App analytics and crash reporting Firebase Privacy
Apple App Store App distribution and payments Apple Privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregate data with:

  • Analytics providers to improve our services
  • Legal authorities if required by law

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Analytics Opt-Out

You can limit analytics collection by:

  • Enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in your iOS Settings
  • Disabling analytics in the App settings (if available)

6.2 Data Deletion

Since we don't store your personal data on our servers:

  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data
  • To request deletion of analytics data, contact us at the email below

6.3 Access Your Data

You can request information about any data we may have associated with your use of the App by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

SnapScreen is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that analytics data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion. Since we collect minimal data and no personal information from your images, these rights are inherently protected.

11. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: For analytics to improve the App
  • Contract: To provide the services you've subscribed to

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data, and to object to or restrict processing.
